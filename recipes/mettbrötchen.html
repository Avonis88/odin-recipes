Mettbrötchen

Description

We are makeing a Delicious german Raw meat food called Mettbrötchen

It is very simple to do and its very Delicious.

Ingredients

  • Bread roll
  • Mett
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cut the Onion in small pieces
  2. Slice the Bread roll
  3. Use a Knife to put Mett on every half of the Bread roll
  4. Push the Bread roll with the Mett side into the sliced Onion
