Lasagna

Description

We are going to cook a wonderful crispy Lasagna.

It is going the be a easy homemade recipe that everyone can do!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Carrots
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the Onions and Carrots
  2. Add the tomato sauce in a pot and cook it
  3. Add the Onions and Carrots to the sauce
  4. Put everything on the plate and put it for 30 min. in the stove
