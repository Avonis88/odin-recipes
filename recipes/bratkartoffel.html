Bratkartoffel

Description

We are going to make crispy Potatoes

Its a common way in germany to cook Potatoes like that.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (sticky)
  • Onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the Potatoes and cut them
  2. Put the cut Potatoes into water to remove the starch
  3. Add some Oil in the Pan add the Potatoes and fry them for around 35 minutes.
  4. After 25 minutes add a cut Onion in a extra pan for around 5 minutes and then add them to the Potatoes.
  5. Add some Salt and Pepper as needed and its done
